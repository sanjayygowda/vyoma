About VYOMA

VYOMA, meaning "sky" or "space" in Sanskrit, is RV University's premier space exploration and astronomy club. We are a community of passionate students dedicated to unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos through observation, research, and hands-on projects.


From stargazing sessions to building model rockets, from astrophotography to space mission simulations, VYOMA offers a platform for students to explore their passion for space science and technology. Join us as we reach for the stars together!
